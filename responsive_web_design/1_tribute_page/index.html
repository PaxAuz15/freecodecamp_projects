The best father in the world

Short stories about the life of Luis Auz

  • My father was very naughty from a young age. He has escaped from killer cows and from escaping death by drowning in a fast-flowing river. But always getting away with a big smile at the end of living crazy experiences.
  • He has had the opportunity to listen to Julio Jaramillo live: on a recording set, among friends and a few good beers. He told me it was an unforgettable experience; he always knew he would become a music legend.
  • He was also a person who enjoyed making neighborhood friends. Every evening they would get together with a radio, listen to music while sharing conversations and bitter jokes over a few beers.
My father does not have social networks but if you want to know about him you can contact me, his son, Luis Auz.
